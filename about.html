Tech 4 Social Good Day is hosted by Tech & Society, a working group formed last year to help foster a community of critical inquiry at ITP and within the tech community in general. Through weekly meetings, reading groups, and guest speakers, we are working to prefigure a technological future that prioritizes ethics and justice and that better serves a wider societal demographic. The Tech 4 Social Good Day conference was a crazy idea to bring together the broader NYU tech community and its adjacents to spend a whole day and a half together to figure out what that might look like. We stand at a vital middle ground between emerging technology, civil society, and culture. We are hoping that you the participants will use this opportunity to meet each other, learn new skills, center marginalized identities, and imagine fresh ways to incorporate ethics into your work.
